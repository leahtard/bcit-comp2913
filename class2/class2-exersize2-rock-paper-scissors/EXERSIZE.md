### Exercise 2 - Rock Paper Scissors

1. Display 3 images, for “Rock”, “Paper” and “Scissors”.
2. Display a score board. Player and Computer start at 0.
3. Display a button to reset the game. This should set both players to score 0.
4. When clicking on the one of the images, your application should pick one of the 3 choices as well, at random.
5. Increment the score by 1 to the winner. Either use an alert or display a message about who won.
6. Once one of the players reaches 5 points, display a message about who won.
